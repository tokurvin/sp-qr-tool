How the check characters are calculated

Check chars are computed over the path only — e.g. POFI-00088285-SMAPLP1003-01-05-03-0010 — regardless of whether a URL prefix is present. That's why v4.0 and v4.1 produce identical check chars for the same data.

Character values: 0–9 → 0–9   A–Z → 10–35   - → 36

Check char 1 (multiplier 31):  acc = (acc × 31 + char_value) mod 37
Check char 2 (multiplier 17):  acc = (acc × 17 + char_value) mod 37

Output alphabet (37 symbols, prime modulus): A–Z (0–25) · 0–9 (26–35) · $ (36)

Error detection: 100% for single-char errors and adjacent transpositions · 99.93% for any two wrong chars.
